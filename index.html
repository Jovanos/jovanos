Join the community

Democraciv is a community dedicated to playing a singleplayer game of Sid Meier's Civilization V using democratic principles. You can find us on Reddit and on Discord.

Immersive Roleplay

The actual game is just a small part of the whole Democraciv expierence! Take part in various political parties, a fully functioning government, the press, different instuituions and universities. 

Don't know where to start?

The Democraciv Foundation provides many tutorials on how to participate in Democraciv. We even have a Wiki if you strive for more!
