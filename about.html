About Us

The Democraciv Foundation was founded on the 27th of July 2017 with the aim to make the Democraciv project an overall more-pleasant experience for everyone, but especially for newcomers.

With an integrated Wiki, different YouTube tutorials and a place for everything Democraciv-related, The Democraciv Foundation is the first institution to bring this much information to the masses easy of access.

DerJonas (u/Jovanos), the founder of The Democraciv Foundation, joined the project when Democraciv had its most members in November 2016.
